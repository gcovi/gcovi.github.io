Appa the flying bison

Appa is the last flying bison. Nations that have seen a flying bison in last 100 years are:

  • Fire nation
  • Earth nation
  • Water nation

Since Aang is the last airbender that has been seen in last 100 years, the Air nation have seen Appa.

Appa is the last of his kind.

Flying bison traits

  • White fur
  • Brown arrow on fur
  • Big pink tongue
  • Cute eyes
